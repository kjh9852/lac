LAC is more
than just an avatar

LAC is a collection of 10,000 Lady Ape NFTs.
300 of these NFTs will be in an exclusive auction
for whitelisted members. Meanwhile, 100 of these
NFTs will be airdopped to 100 Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC)
holders who own the most expensive apes.

LAC BOSS

Chief executive and mastermind

We have detailed milestones set in place to show
that we are here for the long run.

Diamonds

LAC's unique utility token is the ‘Diamond’.
These have no real market
value outside of LAC.
Holders can earn ‘Diamonds’ through
missions and use them to purchase
items through the LAC Shop.

LAC shop

A store with various content for LAC holders.
Holders can use Diamonds to purchase
items in the LAC Shop.

    Social Community Mission

  1. 1. Accept Discord Invite and Follow Twitter

    When you join the official Lady Ape Club (LAC) Discord channel and at the same time verify your
    Twitter followers on Discord, you will receive 150 Diamonds.

  2. 2. Celebrating the LAC Follower Achievement

    If you retweet the post celebrating LAC's achievement of hitting 500/1000/1500 followers,
    you will receive 50 Diamonds for every follower who congratulated you.
    This does not apply to private accounts.

  3. 3. Celebrating the LAC NFT Holder Achievement

    LAC's offcial Twitter account will post milestones such ad reaching 500/1000/1500 total
    holders of LAC NFTs. If you retweet the official post and congratulate LAC for reaching the
    said achievements. you will receive 100 Diamonds. This does not apply to private accounts

  4. 4. Special Missions

    To be announced later on Twitter

    Recommended Missions

  1. 1. Refer a friend

    If your friend becomes an LAC NFT holder after your Invitation
    you can get 200 Diamonds, while your referral will receive 100 Diamonds.
    Plus, if your referral is a Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC) NFT holder,
    he or she can get an extra 50 Diamonds. (150 total)

    PFP Mission

  1. 1. PFP Certification

    Change your Twitter profile to your LAC NFT and you will receive 200 Diamonds

Powder Room

A dressing table that contains
mystery tickets. The price is
yet to be determined.

Key

The key is used to open the
Powder Room. The price is yet
to be determined.

Mystery Ticket

A Mystery Ticket allows holders
to participate in various upcoming
content. The tickets can be foundin
the Powder Room.

Jewelry Box

A Jewelry Box is required to
breed Baby Apes when used together
with DNA (similar to an incubator).
Each Jewelry Box is sold for
600 Diamond Tokens.

Breeding Baby Apes

Baby Apes are divided into three tiers:
Silver, Gold, and Diamond.
Tier Grades are given randomly when
bred using ‘DNA’ and a ‘Jewelry Box’.

BAYC Holder Event

100 people holding the most
expensive BAYCs will each receive
1 LAC for free. Holders will
be rewarded automatically
with Super DNA.

DNA

A DNA is required to breed
Baby Apes when used together with
aJewelry Box. Each DNA is sold for
300 Diamond Tokens.

Super DNA

Super DNA has a higher chance to
breed high-tier Baby Apes and this is
only offered to BAYC holders.
Breeding can be done after
purchasing a Jewelry Box
required for breeding.
